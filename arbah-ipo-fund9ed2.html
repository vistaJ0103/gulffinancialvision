Our Businesses Arbah IPO Fund

Arbah IPO Fund

The objective of the fund is to achieve a return exceeding the performance of the Arbah S&P IPO Index, by investing in Saudi IPOs and Saudi companies listed for five years or less. The fund manager can also invest in other funds which have similar objectives and goals.

The investment strategy is to invest assets in a diversified portfolio comprised of Shariah-compliant companies listed on the Saudi Stock Exchange, using a multi-strategy approach.
