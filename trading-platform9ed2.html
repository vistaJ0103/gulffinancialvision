Trading Platform

Our Trader Workstation (TWS) and Pro 9 platforms are designed for our most sophisticated clients, giving active traders professional trading capabilities equal to any top brokerage in the world. TWS allows for instant transactions, with live streaming prices and instant market updates, enabling Gulf Wealth Trading clients to profit from price fluctuations and market volatility. This is complemented by the Pro 9 application, which allows access to a wide range of statistical and analytical data for accurate analysis and research.
